Stealth Accessibility

Stealth noun (stelθ)

  1. movement that is quiet and careful in order not to be seen or heard, or secret action
  2. surreptitious; secret; not openly acknowledged

Accessibility noun (ɪkˈses·əˈbɪl·ɪ·t̬i)

  1. the fact of being able to be reached or obtained easily
  2. the quality of being easy to understand

Stealth a11y

Secretly fixing stuff in such a way that everybody can easily reach, obtain and understand it
